On the north side is the realm of the transhumanists,  who hold a neo-Darwinian worldview and pursue ideals which might to  some seem like an extension of the Enlightenment. On the south side are the posthumanists: postmodernists chary of technology and bemused by the lack of aesthetic sensibilities among those across the water. (Latorra 532**

Transhumanists and posthumanists are somewhat at odds with respect  to their use of terminology (Latorra 532**

Many transhumanists imagine that our ultimate future—our truly posthuman future—can only reach its maximum potential when our minds are freed from their fleshy brains and re-instantiated in cyberspace, where space, time, and matter are all malleable and subject to our will (Latorra 533**
