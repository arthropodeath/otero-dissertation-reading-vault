Huxley, Julian. New Bottles for New Wine. Chatto & Windus, London, 1957


About [[Terminology/Transhumanism\|Transhumanism]]

This cosmic self-awareness is being realized in one tiny fragment of the universe – in a few of us human beings (Huxley 13)

Something wholly new and revolutionary, human beings with their capacities for conceptual thought and language, for self-conscious awareness and purpose, for accumulating and pooling conscious experience. (Huxley 13)

**Man’s responsibility and destiny – to be an agent for the rest of the world in the job of realizing its inherent potentialities as fully as possible. (Huxley 13)

**Prepare itself for the cosmic office (Huxley 14)

We need to explore and map the whole realm of human possibility (Huxley 15)

>“man remaining man, but transcending himself, by realizing new possibilities of and for his human nature. (Huxley 17)**
