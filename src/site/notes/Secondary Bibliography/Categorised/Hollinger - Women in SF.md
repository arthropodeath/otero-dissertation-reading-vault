Women writers, readers, and critics have exercised a powerful influence on the development of SF over the last three decades; and SF by women, whether or not it defines itself as feminist, has contributed both to the growing sophistication of the genre and to its increasing heterogeneity Hollinger women 129

SF by women, in spite of an initial-and in some cases continuing-resistance from within an already well-established field, has itself become an established presence, redefining the field in the process and, concurrently, becoming itself redefined. Hollinger women  129
