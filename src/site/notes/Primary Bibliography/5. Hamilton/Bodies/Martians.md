They are humans, geneered (modified by [[Terminology/Embodied as Biological\|Embodied as Biological]] means) to live on the moon and, later, in Mars. They are [[Terminology/Embodied as Transhuman\|Embodied as Transhuman]] in nature but carry more of a  [[Terminology/Embodied as Posthuman\|Embodied as Posthuman]] ideology since they modified themselves for the environment rather than vice-versa.
