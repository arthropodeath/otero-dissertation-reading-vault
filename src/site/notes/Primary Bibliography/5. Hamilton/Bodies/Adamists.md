[[Terminology/Embodied as Transhuman\|Embodied as Transhuman]] sub-group of the human species with both [[Terminology/Embodied as Cybernetic\|Embodied as Cybernetic]] and [[Terminology/Embodied as Biological\|Embodied as Biological]] methodology, although the former is more widespread and the latter is mostly based on genetic improvements such as longer lifespans and attractiveness.
